Fish Haven Community Gardens hands out food as part of Giving Tuesday

Community Gardens spent its Giving Tuesday by giving food to those in need. It's part of its 10,000 Pound Food Giveaway. Throughout the day, the group went to different locations in DeKalb County to hand out fresh and non-perishable food. Founder and Executive Director Dan Kenney hopes that by giving all of this food, the group can help make things a little easier for others.

"Hopefully, it will alleviate some of their stress and they can use some of their money to pay their rent, pay their medical bills, things like that, so that we can have the food available to them so that they can take some of their pressure off of them," said Kenney.
