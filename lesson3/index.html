Site Purpose

The primary purpose of this site is to provide users with local weather information based upon their current location in Eastern Idaho and surrounding areas. The information, gathered from satellites and weather balloons from vaious organizations, will be displayed on screen by search specific request or available through an interactive map.

A secondary purpose of this site is to provide details of local weather related news reports by relevent news stations. Details on news stories and inofrmation will be provided in a clean, user friendly manner.
