Target Audience

The intended audience of WeatherWeekly.com is for individuals in the Eastern Idaho area that desire to know the forecast or upcoming weather events. The areas located in the weather forecast zone include several towns and one univeristy, and will thus be targeted to commuters and college students.

Personas

Susan T. Marshall

Demographics: 53 years old, married, college education, works 7 miles from home and drives there every week day.

Occupation: Susan is a high school teacher in a local town.

Goals: Know the weather on a hourly/daily/weekly basis so she knows what the driving conditions will be like in the winter. She also would like to plan ahead on her weekends to spend time at Bear Lake with her family.

Website Use: She mainly uses her desktop at home and at work.

William Rassy

Demographics: 29 years old, dating, college education, lives locally near Preston.

Occupation: Assistant Operations Manager for an online outdoor goods company.

Goals: As an avid hiker, he needs to know weather conditions for the surrounding area to plan for hikes and other excursions.

Website Use: He uses his iPhone and occasionally his work computer to look up the weather report.
