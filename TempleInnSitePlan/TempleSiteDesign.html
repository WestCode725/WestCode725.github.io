GRAND SPIRES Website Design Plan

Site Purpose

The goal of the GRAND SPIRES website will be to make the process of reserving places of shelter for those away from home eaiser than ever to do. With hopes that customers will choose this hotel over any others, many amenities including food, refreshment, play-rooms and babysitting services, kitchenette suites, wedding halls, and other services will be provided and shown on the home page.

Target Audience

The intended audience of GRAND SPIRES is mainly for individuals who work in temples located Pennsylvania, Virginia, Washington D.C., and North Carolina. All those who work and travel long distances to do work may require a room and other amenities.
