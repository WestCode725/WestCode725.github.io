Small magnitude 3.5 quake hits near Soda Springs, Idaho early morning

PThe United States Geological Survey (USGS) reported a magnitude 3.5 quake in the Idaho near the town of Soda Springs only 14 minutes ago. The earthquake hit early morning on Sunday 22 November 2020 at 4.04 am local time at a very shallow depth of 4 miles. The exact magnitude, epicenter, and depth of the quake might be revised within the next few hours or minutes as seismologists review data and refine their calculations, or as other agencies issue their report.

A second report was later issued by the European-Mediterranean Seismological Centre (EMSC), which listed it as a magnitude 3.5 earthquake as well.
